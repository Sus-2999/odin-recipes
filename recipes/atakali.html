ATAKALI

DESCRIPTION

This dessert is made by using milk and contains small marble-sized balls made up of rice.

Ingredients

  • Raw rice - 150 g
  • Sugar - 75 g
  • Scraped coconut - 1 cup
  • Black pepper powder - Half teaspoon
  • Salt according to taste

Steps

  1. Soak the rice in water for about an hour. After soaking, separate rice from water and allow it to dry.
  2. Grind the dried rice into fine powder.
  3. Take one and a half cup water in a container and heat it on medium flame.
  4. Add black pepper powder, salt and half the amount of scraped coconut into the water. Stir the mixture well.
  5. Slowly add half of the rice powder into the mixture while continuously stirring it on flame.
  6. Stop stirring when you obtain a thick dough. Turn off the flame and allow the dough to cool down.
  7. After the dough is cooled enough, make little balls of the dough.
  8. Add just enough water to the remaining rice powder so that a rice paste is formed.
  9. Take six cups of water in another container and heat it on medium flame.
  10. Add sugar, salt and remaining scraped coconut to the boiling water and stir it continuously to form syrup.
  11. Little by little, add the rice paste to the boiling syrup and continue stirring.
  12. Then add the dough balls into the syrup and stir the contents for 30 seconds on low flame.
  13. Turn off the flame. Let the contents cool down.
  14. Your Atakali dessert is ready to be served.
