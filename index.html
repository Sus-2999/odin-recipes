ODIN RECIPES

Odisha, formerly Orissa, is an Indian state located in east India. It has a rich culture with vast diversity in flora, fauna, traditions and also food.
Odisha cuisine is exquisite among all the Indian cuisines available in the sense that it uses local ingredients available almost all the time and brings out the natural flavour of those ingredients, which blend with the spices leading to mouth-watering tastes.

The Odia cuisine has its fair share of popular desserts across India. Some of them are listed below. Go explore the dessert world of Odisha and enjoy the sublime feelings of joy. HAPPY JOURNEY !!!!

  • Atakali
  • Khiri
  • Rasagola
